## Email-Specific Rules

Apply these ONLY when writing email copy.

### Structural Rules

**Greetings:**
- First email in sequence: `Hey {{name}},`
- Follow-up emails: `{{name}},` (no "Hey")

**Paragraphs:**
- 1-3 sentences max per paragraph
- One thought per paragraph
- Generous whitespace

**Lists:**
- 3-5 items max
- One item per line
- NO bullet points or dashes â€” just line breaks

**Emphasis:**
- Use `â†’` before the primary CTA (ONE per email max)
- NO bold in email body
- Use spacing for emphasis instead

**Sign-offs:**
- In sequences: "Talk [day of week]." where [day] = the weekday the next email sends
- Standalone emails: "Looking forward to it." / "Talk soon." / "See you tomorrow."
- Always just "Artem" (no dash)

### Rhythm & Pacing

1. **One sentence per line.** Break compound sentences apart.
2. **Use ellipsis (...)** for trailing thoughts. 1-2 per email max.
3. **Use conversational interjections as transitions:** "The second type though?" / "Solid, right??" / "And here's the craziest part:"
4. **Colons before lists/explanations.** "Here's what I mean:" not "Here's what I mean."
5. **Never echo subject/preview in the opening.** Jump straight into content.
6. **Cut aggressively.** Remove setup sentences, transition phrases, repeated conclusions.
7. **Double question marks** ("??") for rhetorical emphasis.

### Subject Lines

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Direct question | "Quick question before our call" |
| Specific number | "The $2K/video question" |
| Curiosity gap | "One thing I forgot to mention" |
| Time-based | "12 hours from now" |
| Pattern interrupt | "This might sound weird" |

Subject lines: 4-8 words, lowercase (unless proper noun), no periods, personal not promotional.

### Preview Text

- 40-90 characters
- Complements subject line (doesn't repeat it)
- Creates curiosity or continues the thought
- Written as a fragment, not a complete sentence

### Email Output Format

```
**Subject:** [subject line]
**Preview:** [preview text]

---

[Email body]
```

### Sequence Rules

- Don't repeat offer details covered in earlier emails. Each email adds NEW information or a new angle.
- When a checkout/landing page exists, make that the primary CTA. Don't use "reply and I'll send the link" when the link exists.
- First mention of a program's price should use the full program name. After that, shorthand is fine.
